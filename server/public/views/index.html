Tracy Fuller - Artist, Designer, Metalsmith, Nerd

I've been obsessed with jewelry ever since my Grandmother bought me my first pair of pearl earrings to celebrate when I turned 10, even though I didn't have pierced ears yet! They had such a beautiful luster and I would stare endlessly at how the light would create a pinky sheen on the white pearls.

It wasn't long before I asked for my first set of tools and started teaching myself how to create the designs that I would imagine myself wearing. Natural stones, semi-precious gems, Swarovski crystals and precious metals are my favorite materials to use. I am not satisfied until a design is something that I would be proud to wear.

I welcome inquiries about my work! You can reach me through either Etsy store

ForgeLakeStudio.etsy.com

With my Forge Lake Studio designs, I forge precious metals, gemstones and glass into wearable works of art. From the simple and everyday styles to dramatic statement pieces, my designs appeal to many fashion styles. Your wardrobe may change, but you'll always find something fun and beautiful to perk up and add detail to every look.

BitsMonster.etsy.com

Some bits have byte! Have you ever looked at the breadboard of the circuits that run your life? All those shiny little information highways running across a field of green, or blue, or red. They're just plain cool. I was sitting in my mundane life in the tech industry when I found that I really wanted a way to show off my inner computer nerd. A chance find of some circuit boards that were being thrown away gave me the idea. Why not make jewelry out of them?

I now try to source all of my circuit boards from recycled and reclaimed sources. Some were overstock, and headed for a landfill, some came out of junked computers, and all had a story to tell.
